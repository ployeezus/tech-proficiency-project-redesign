About Us

The Bloomington Bagel Company is downtown Bloomington's first on-site made from scratch bagel bakery! BBC Bagels are boiled and baked right here every day, using only the highest quality ingredients, following kosher recipes from New York City's historic Bagel Bakers Local #338! Try our "Can't Be Copied" kettle-boiled hearth-baked bagels today!
